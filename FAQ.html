Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to be Latino to join ALPFA-Rutgers Newark?

Absolutely not! Our organizaition prides itself for its vast diveristy with 30% of our members hailing from non-Hispanic backgrounds. Any student of Rutgers-Newark, regardless of their race, creed, nationality, religion, etc. is encouraged to apply & become a part of the ALPFAfamilia!.

Is this club only tailored for Accounting & Finance students?

This is a common misconception that hailed from ALPFA originally standing for "American Latino Professions in Finance & Accounting". However, as time progressed, in 2010, the name was changed to "American Latino Professionals for America" to be more inclusive to professionals from all industries. Our club is most certainly welcome to any major that wants to develop their leadership skills & professional development. We have partnered up with numerous companies from a variety of areas such as technology, marketing, etc.

What is a committee and how do I join one?

One of the greatest assets to our club are the existence of committees. In the variety of our divisions, we have commmittees led by the Vice Presidents in charge of them. A member can apply to these committees and gain skills relevant to them, as well as leadership skills



Applications to become a committee member are usually released in early September.

How will meetings be conducted when the univeristy is virtual for the Fall 2020 semester?

To accomodate with this virtual environment, we will conduct our meetings via Zoom. Each member will receive updates on upcoming events along with the links to access them if they are registered to our newsletter. These meetings will be 100% interactive & fun, we will ensure that all our members are left with a pleasurable experience from attending them.

What are the costs of membership?

There is no cost associated with becoming an ALPFA-Rutgers Newark member. Once you sign up to us and the official ALPFA website, you're in. There is only a $30 if you want to convert your ALPFA Membership from Student to Professional, which will provide you with exclusive content from the official ALPFA organization,
