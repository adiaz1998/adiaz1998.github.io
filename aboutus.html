Who Are We?

About Us

The Association of Latino Professionals for America (ALPFA) was the first national Latino professional organization in the United States with its first chapter established in Los Angeles, California. This nonprofit organization was created in 1972 with one mission in mind: To empower and develop Latino men and women as leaders of character for the nation, in every sector of the global economy. They provide members scholarships, career fairs, networking events and mentorship opportunities. Serving over 92,000 professional and student members across the country, ALPFA currently has 160 active professional chapters and 45 student chapters.
