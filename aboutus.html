About Us

Our Mission

The Association of Latino Professionals for America (ALPFA) was the first national Latino professional organization in the United States with its first chapter established in Los Angeles, California. This nonprofit organization was created in 1972 with one mission in mind:

To empower & develop minorities as leaders of character for the nation in every sector of the global economy

Who Are We?

We transform students to business professionals through empowering relationships, continuous learning, and leadership growth that creates a lasting impact to their respective communities.

What Do We Do?

We help students kick start their careers by guiding, training, and inform them of opportunities and the opportunity to make connections. We also have the reputation as being one of the most hardworking and accomplished students on campus.
