Bienvenidos to the ALPFA Familia!

Why You Should Join!

ALPFA Brand

ALPFA-RUN has won multiple awards ranging from student organization of the year to the best chapter in the northeast region. ALPFA Rutgers-Newark serves the general population with the utter purpose to develop professionals through the means of acquiring technical skills, analytical skills, and professional skills by governing and being involved in a student organization which undertakes the responsibilities as a real-life business entity.

Professsional Devleopment

ALPFA Rutgers-Newark prides itself by being an organization that provides its members with the necessary skills needed to succeed in the professional world.

One of the greatest assets that we possess are the existence of committees. From Marketing, Information Technology, Consulting, Event Planning, Finance, Professional Development, etc. Each of members have the opportunity to become participants of these task-forces & work along in a team governed by its of each respective function.

Our members can take advantage of this resource that we have and acquire skills from web development (IT), case studies (Consulting), social media marketing (Marketing), budgeting (Finance), and much more. These are valuable experiences that employers seek nowadays and our organization comes bundled with essential assets critical to your success!

Not only that, however, our club also comes with exclusive events intended for professional development. For instance, we have just launched our Tech series, which is composed of weekly technical workshops that teach our members hard skills. We have also frequent case study workshops, resume-building workshops, and our Mentor & Mentee program that pairs students with professionals in their respective field of interest for 5-months.

Expand Your Network

Ever since its beginnings in 1972 in Los Angeles, ALPFA has grown tremendously with more than 95,000+ professional and student members. It is a nationally recongized organization with immense prestigue and partnerships with over 100 Fortune 500 companies. Our student members can take advantage of this vast network and connect with professionals from a variety of industries. From the events that we host to the ones hosted by the ALPFA national organization to internship opportunities posted on the official website, our members can reap these benefits to their full advantage. After all, it is estimated that 80% of jobs are filled through networking!

Participate in the Regional Symposium & National Convetion!

Arguably, the most important events that ALPFA hosts are the 2 regional symposiums and the national convention. The regional symposiums are hosted in the Fall & Spring, respectively, and function as a full-blown career fair with 20+ corporate sponsors. Students can network with professionals, highly-motivated students, and acquire full-blown internships or full-time offers!

However, the most prominent is the National Convention which is held every August. This convention is usually held in lavish locations in key locations in the US. It's a 5-day experience accompanied with 50+ corporate sponsors that are willing to interview on spot! In fact, several of our past members have received internship or full-time offers from prestigious companies from this way. In the convention, there are also an abundance of workshops hosted by companies themselves and a perfect opportunity to network with professionals or like-minded students like yourself. As a club, we provide scholarships for our hardest working members at the end of the year to go to this remarkable event!

Become a part of the ALPFamilia!

Here at ALPFA Rutgers-Newark, we are not only just a professional organization, but a community itself. We take pride in helping eachother out and building lifelong bonds. Our general body meetings are entertaining and hosted in a friendly & cordial manner. We have end-of-semester parties and we give back to our community through volunteering efforts. Therfore, by joining our club, you are not just enrolling to a typical student club that is used a filler for your resume, but a component to our ALPFamilia.
