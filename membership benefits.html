Welcome to the ALPFA Familia!

At ALPFA Rutgers-Newark we care about you. We know the dedicated, hard-working students we have on our campus and want to provide the resources you need to help shape your future. We are here to motivate and develop you into professionals. By offering direct access to opportunities and support, we will supply the technical, analytical, and professional skills needed to excel in Corporate America. Our diverse and inclusive environment is a space for you to grow and be a part of the ALPFAmilia.

Let us be the bridge between where you are now, and where you see yourself in the future.

Melody Mejia

VP of Member Relations

I transferred to Rutgers Business School towards the end of my sophomore year. Two weeks into the semester, I became an ALPFA member. I didn’t do it because of the amazing resources available to its members; I did it because I thought it would be an excellent opportunity to make friends. Not only did I make friends, but I also became a part of the ALPFAmilia. My favorite thing about ALPFA was how diverse AND inclusive the environment is. Later on, I decided to join the consulting committee, where I learned soft and analytical skills. Now I am on the 2020-2021 Executive Board! I also got an internship with EY through ALPFA for summer 2020 and was offered to return for summer 2021! This organization allowed me to pursue many opportunities, and I want the same for YOU.

We pride ourselves in getting you acclimated to make the first interaction with your ideal employer and walking away with confidence.

ALPFA is a national organization that serves both students and professionals with 95,000+ members. This allows ALPFA to form partnerships with over 100 Fortune 500 companies. As a member, you become a part of this rich network.

Unlock access to ALPFA’s regional symposiums and national convention. These are the most anticipated events of the year and we highly encourage our members to attend. These events function as career fairs where our students get the opportunity to interview on the spot. Our past members have landed internships and full-time offers in top firms like Goldman Sachs, EY, and Accenture. We want YOU to be in the front-lines of these opportunities.

To receive direct benefits to attend these events and be a part of our community, log on to alpfa.org and register to be a member. Check out the benefits of each package below: If you have any questions, reach out to us directly.
